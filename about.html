Our Story

At Yolk Empire, we believe in delivering the highest quality eggs directly from local farms to your doorstep. Started with a mission to provide affordable, fresh eggs to Kenyan families, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide the freshest, most affordable eggs in Kenya, supporting local farmers and bringing our customers the best of farm-fresh produce. We aim to be the go-to supplier for all things eggs, whether you're buying for home use or your business.

Our Vision

To grow our business nationwide and provide reliable delivery of farm-fresh eggs across the country while maintaining our focus on supporting sustainable, local agriculture.

Our Process

We partner with farmers who prioritize quality and care in their production methods. After the eggs are collected, they go through a strict quality check before being packaged and delivered to you.

Meet the Team
